Hi, I'm Shannon

I am a Board Certified Nurse Coach with my own private practice based out of New York. I have been a nurse for five years at a trauma center hospital. I have noticed patterns while working in my field, and I have come to know that there is a way to prevent more people from suffering and struggling alone in our current healthcare system. I believe every human is WHOLE and with the right support, they too can step into the life they desire to live.

My work now as a nurse and health coach is primarily focused on sustained behavioral change addressing the physical, mental, emotional, and spiritual. I have helped clients of all ages break down barriers to change, co-create action steps and keep them accountable to their goals during our time together. I offer one FREE “discovery call” for you to experience this work, and from there if the shoe fits, we commit to working together over the course of three months! Three weeks on, one week off.

I am excited to get to know each of you, and am ready to meet you right where you are at on your health and wellness journey!
